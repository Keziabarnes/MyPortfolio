My Summary of The Twelve Pillars Chapters 4-7

In Chapters 4 through 7 of Twelve Pillars, I learned four powerful lessons that can transform my life. Chapter 4 taught me that goals are not just about achieving destinations but about who I become in the process, writing down my goals and breaking them into manageable steps that will shape my character and discipline. Chapter 5 revealed that I must choose between the pain of discipline now or the pain of regret later, every day is expensive, so I must focus on what's truly important rather than what's merely urgent. Chapter 6 challenged me to carefully evaluate my associations, recognizing that the people I surround myself with either lift me toward my goals or pull me away from them. Finally, Chapter 7 emphasized that formal education makes a living, but self-education makes a fortune,becoming a lifelong learner through reading, observing successful people, and reflecting on my experiences is essential
